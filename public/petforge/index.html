Core Features

Six pillars of the PetForge experience

🔨

FORGE

Create unique pets from 6 elemental materials. Every combination yields a different creature with its own stats and abilities.

⚔️

BATTLE

3v3 turn-based combat with PvP arenas and massive World Boss raids. Strategy matters more than level.

🧬

EVOLVE

Pets evolve based on YOUR playstyle. Aggressive fighters become fierce beasts; healers bloom into guardians.

🌍

LIVING WORLD

Dynamic weather, random events, and a full day/night cycle that changes which pets you can find and forge.

💎

COLLECT

30+ pets across 8 rarity tiers up to CELESTIAL. Fill your bestiary and show off your rarest catches.

🏆

COMPETE

50 achievements, global leaderboards, and a seasonal pass with exclusive rewards every month.
