An Ocean of Possibilities

We have explored and documented 8 unique spots in the Lakes Region. Each day, our team chooses the best destination based on the forecast and your skill level, guaranteeing 4 days of epic riding.

The Adventure

This isn't a tour; it's an expedition. The itinerary is a guide, but the conditions rule.

Day 0: The Beginning

We welcome you at Puerto Montt airport (PMC). In the evening, we gather for a welcome dinner, where you'll meet the guides, go over our safety protocols, and analyze the forecast for the upcoming days.

Days 1-4: The Wind Hunt

The daily routine is simple: we wake up, analyze the wind map, and move to the spot with the best conditions. It could be a flat-water session in front of a volcano at Llanquihue, a wave-riding session in the Pacific at Carelmapu, or a memorable downwinder in the Reloncaví fjord. The mission is to ride the best 4 spots Patagonia has to offer during your stay.

The Nights: Rest & Strategy

After each session, we return to our home base to rest, enjoy a barbecue, good wine, and share the day's stories. We analyze footage and plan the strategy for the next day.

Day 5: The Farewell

Depending on flight schedules and conditions, a final morning session is possible. We end with a farewell lunch before taking you to the airport, with a full memory card and new friendships.
